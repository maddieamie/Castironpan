Personality Quiz: What kind of artist creature are you?

Are you new to drawing? Interested in what kind of artist you may be? Take our quiz below to see what kind of artist creature you are!

Question 1: What getaway vacation appeals to you the most?

Question 2: What do you consider to be your defining trait?

Question 3: What is your favorite kind of food?

Question 4: What genre of art appeals to you the most?

Question 5: What's a favorite thing you enjoy doing?

Question 6: Which "adult beverage" is your preferred? [if you're underage, just pretend you know what we are talking about... or go with what sounds pretty :)]

Question 7: What would you do if you could stop time?

Question 8: What hobbies do you enjoy, or would like to try, outside of art?

Question 9: What is your favorite genre of media?

Question 10: What's your Dungeons & Dragons/ RPG class?

I hope you enjoy this quiz! If you have suggestions to improve this project, please contact me on the About Me page. I am a new developer and would love constructive feedback. I am particularly interested in feedback from those who experience challenges online: your views and tips will help me improve the product for everyone. Thank you for your time and patience!
