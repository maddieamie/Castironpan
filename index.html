What is the CastIron Pan Mini-Paint App?

The idea for this app is to have a tiny painting application with interesting color palettes to choose from, alongside a color-chooser. The main objectives of this app is for you, the user, to be able to:

  • Use pre-made Color Palettes that work well with various visual needs + have a fun aesthetic
  • Reload the page without losing your current project
  • Save the images you make to your device
  • Future Goal: Work on mobile!

I hope you enjoy this mini application! If you have suggestions to improve this project, please contact me on the About Me page. I am a new developer and would love constructive feedback. I am particularly interested in feedback from those who experience challenges online: your views and tips will help me improve the product for everyone. Thank you for your time and patience!
